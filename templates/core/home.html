    ABOUT ALCHERINGA

    Alcheringa is the annual cultural festival of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati. Started in 1996 by a group of students, Alcheringa, IIT Guwahati has become one of Asiaâ€™s most significant college-level cultural fests. Spread over three days and four nights, Alcheringa attracts people from all over the world with its spectacular events and competitions.
